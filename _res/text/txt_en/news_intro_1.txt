The Central Committee announces a competitive selection process for the position of Director in the newly established Mine No 147.
 Candidates are hereby instructed to submit applications by postal service to the Department of Mining and Fossil Fuels.
 The application package must include: personal biography, school certificates, certified copy of the birth certificate, copy of the national identification card, copy of the Party membership card with a photo, copy of the military service book, diploma of higher education, police clearance certificate issued and signed by the commanding officer of the relevant district of the People's Militia, four passport-sized photographs, payment receipt for the administrative processing fee in the amount of 25,000 zlotys.
