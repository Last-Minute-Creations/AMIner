We bring you the latest news from the Motherland.
The First Secretary, accompanied by local Party officials, took part in the harvest festival celebrations in the village of Wolka Oborno.
In an address to the assembled farmers and rural workers, the First Secretary emphasized the immeasurable benefits of collectivization and mechanization of agriculture and solemnly pledged the assistance of the People's Army during this year's harvest.
The Youth Labor Brigades have likewise declared their unwavering commitment to supporting this noble endeavor.
